# PRIVACY POLICY

**Last updated:** 2025-11-18

This Privacy Policy explains how **Invoice Builder** (“the Application”) handles your information.  
The Application is designed with privacy in mind and stores all data **locally** on your device.

The developer (“we,” “us,” “our”) does **not** collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data.

By using the Application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. No Data Collection

The Application does **not**:

- collect personal information
- collect usage analytics
- send your data to external servers
- use tracking tools (e.g., Google Analytics)
- store any information outside your device

All information you enter remains **local** and under your control.

---

## 2. Local Data Storage Only

All data-including invoices, clients, businesses, items, settings, and reports-is stored:

- **only on your computer**
- **only in the local application directory, database or docker container**

We do not have access to this data and cannot view, copy, or recover it.

You are solely responsible for:

- backing up your files
- preventing unauthorized access to your device
- maintaining data accuracy

---

## 3. No Cloud Services, No Accounts

The Application:

- does **not** require login or registration
- does **not** connect to a cloud service
- does **not** synchronize data across devices

Any file exports (PDF, backups, etc.) are saved by you in the location you choose.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

If you manually choose to:

- store files in cloud services (Dropbox, OneDrive, Google Drive, etc.)
- print using third-party printer software
- upload exported documents to external platforms

…then those services handle your data under **their** privacy policies.  
We have no control over or responsibility for these actions.

---

## 5. Open Source Notice

The Application is open source.  
Third-party contributors may read or modify the source code, but they do **not** have any access to your local data unless you intentionally share it.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Because we do not collect any information, the Application does not knowingly collect or process data from children.  
If a child uses the Application, no personal data is transferred or stored externally.

---

## 7. Security

The Application does not transmit or manage data, so we cannot access or secure your files.

Security of your data depends fully on:

- your operating system
- your device
- your backups
- your local environment

We strongly recommend using proper device security (passwords, encryption, antivirus, backups).

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Changes will be posted in the repository.  
Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of any updates.
